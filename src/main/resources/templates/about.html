Welcome to Luxury Car Rentals

Luxury Car Rentals was established back in 2007 as one of the first of its kind Luxury rent-a-Car services.

Our initial aim was to create a company where people come, not only to rent a vehicle but also get the best experience possible. We have grown a lot since then and now we also offer a lot more to our customers than just a vehicle you rent for the weekend.

Since we always strive to have the best customer experience, now we offer to you a personal chauffeur, airport transfers and a lot more. We are proud of the services we offer and we are glad to see that so many of you have enjoyed your experience with us.
